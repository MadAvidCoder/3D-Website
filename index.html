Hi.

There is little point to this website, other than to endlessly watch a cube and donut (do not question the donut) endlessly spinning in an endless void of endless endlessness.

This truly inspiring nothingness was created with three.js and javascript. And maybe Vite. And HTML and CSS of course.

Ok, but seriously... What is actually going on here?

As you scroll, the camera pans through an endless universe of nothingness. Because giving doom-scrolling the power to control an entire universe was a great idea. (At the time at least...)

The cube rotates. The donut rotates. Everything rotates. ROTATION IS EVERYWHERE.

Except the stars. They're normal. (I think).
